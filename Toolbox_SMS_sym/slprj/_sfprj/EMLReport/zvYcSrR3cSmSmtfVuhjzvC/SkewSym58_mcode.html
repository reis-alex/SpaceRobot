1function [x_skew] = SkewSym(x)

 4%
 5% [x_skew] = SkewSym(x)
 6%
 7% :parameters:
 8%	* x -- [3x1] column matrix (the vector).

11%	* x_skew -- [3x3] skew-symmetric matrix of x.
